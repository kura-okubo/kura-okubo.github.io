About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the National Research Institute for Earth Science and Disaster Resilience (NIED).

Research Interest

My research focuses on earthquake source mechanics. I have explored this topic through numerical modeling, ambient seismic noise monitoring, and laboratory experiments to clarify the essential processes of earthquake rupture dynamics across different scales. My goal is to unravel earthquake source processes with adequate spatiotemporal resolution, contributing to robust and realistic earthquake rupture scenarios.
