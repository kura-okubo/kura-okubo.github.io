New paper: Monitoring velocity change over 20 years at Parkfield is published.

April 18, 2024

New paper out: “Monitoring Velocity Change Over 20 Years at Parkfield” has been published!

Okubo, K., Delbridge, B. G., and Denolle, M. A. Monitoring velocity change over 20 years at parkfield. J. Geophys. Res., 129(4):e2023JB028084, 2024, doi:10.1029/2023JB028084.

Open research#

We maintain software, a minimal working example, and the Jupyter notebooks in GitHub to reproduce the analysis presented in the manuscript.

  • Seismonitoring.jl: A Julia-based software package to process the ambient seismic noise.
  • SeisMonitoring_Example: A minimal working example of the SeisMonitoring.jl
  • SeisMonitoring_Paper: Jupyter notebooks to post-process the data and to plot figures The intermediate files of the post-processing are available in the UW dasway.
